Multiple triggers

Two controls share one tooltip via data-tooltip-trigger="shared-tip". The first hover waits 400 ms; while the tooltip stays open, moving to the sibling trigger repositions instantly without another delay.

Both controls describe the same underlying action.

Animated positioning

--transform-origin anchors enter and exit transitions to the active trigger. Hover the button near the bottom edge.

Open and close delay

Hover waits 400 ms to open and 150 ms to close. Focus still opens immediately.

Opens after a short hover delay.

Delay group

Sibling hosts share data-tooltip-delay-group. The first hover waits 400 ms; moving to another button while a group tooltip is open switches instantly. While data-tooltip-skip-delay is active, leave also waits so you can cross the gap between buttons.
